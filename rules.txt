 Goal: 
 - You have one month (31 days) to earn as many cake points (CP) as you can get! To earn cake points you must bake cakes! 
 -
 - Rules:
 - The more cakes you bake, the more cake points you will score. 
 - You can not bake a cake without a recipe, which you can purchase at the market.
 - You start with no ingredients in your pantry, but ingredients can be purchased at the market.
 - Money can be earned (or lost) by rolling a dice once a day! Your luck will determine the money you earn or lose - in a day!
 - Player starts with $0 -
